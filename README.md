\# AI Workforce Transformation Analysis



\## Business Problem



Organizations are investing heavily in AI and automation, but leadership teams need evidence that these investments improve business outcomes.



This project analyzes workforce, automation, hiring, layoffs, productivity, and financial performance data to identify relationships between AI adoption and organizational success.



\---



\## Project Goals



\- Evaluate the relationship between AI adoption and revenue growth

\- Analyze workforce changes associated with automation

\- Measure employee satisfaction across AI adoption levels

\- Identify industries benefiting most from AI implementation

\- Develop executive-ready dashboards and recommendations
